## What You'll Learn

This tutorial will cover:

- How to check your vesting schedule
- How to view releasable tokens
- How to claim vested tokens
- Understanding cliff periods
- Understanding conditional vesting based on shop performance

## Prerequisites

Before starting this tutorial, you'll need:

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, etc.)
- A vesting allocation (team, investor, operator, or staker)
- Network gas tokens for transaction fees

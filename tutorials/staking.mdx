## Staking Tiers Comparison

| Tier | Lock Duration | Reward Multiplier | Governance Weight | Best For |
|------|-------------|-------------------|-------------------|----------|
| Flexible | No lock | 1.0x | 1.0x | Short-term holders |
| Bronze | 3 months | 1.25x | 1.25x | Testing staking |
| Silver | 6 months | 1.5x | 1.5x | Medium commitment |
| Gold | 12 months | 2.0x | 2.0x | Long-term believers |

## Staking Strategy Tips

<Tip>
**Pro Tip:** Gold tier (12-month lock) gives you 2x rewards and 2x voting power compared to Flexible tier. If you're long-term aligned, Gold tier maximizes your benefits.
</Tip>
